What is Smart Aurangabad Hackathon?

Smart Aurangabad Hackathon is an advent for thinkers and innovators to design, develop and find smart ways to make world a better place. It’s the aura for ideas and the inventions!

Art makes questions. Design makes solution. Technology makes possibility and Hackathon turns possibility to come in reality. The first edition of our innovation challange invites participants from across the nation to submit sustainable and scalable solutions to challenges from diverse domains.

The task is not so much to see what no one has yet seen, but to think what nobody has yet thought, about that which everybody sees!



The Institution

Government College of Engineering Aurangabad (GECA) is one of the renowned engineering institutions in the country. Situated in Aurangabad city in Maharashtra state, GECA is home to some of the brightest young minds of the country engaged in a wide array of disciplines. With a vibrant and robust student body, GECA has been the launchpad for people with great ideas that have grown to be very successful ventures including CareNX, Jingletoons and others. This year, we celebrate our Diamond Jubilee and are hosting Marathwada's first-ever 'Hackathon' - a pioneer for finding technological solutions to citizens' challenges.

FAQs

1.) Is this hackathon an online event or offline?

-> Smart Aurangabad Hackathon is a combination of offline and online event and as a participant you will need to be present at the Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad campus if you are shortlisted in the first round.

2.) How big can a team be?

-> You can form teams of up to 4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

3.) Is there any registration fee?

-> No. Registrations are completely free of cost.

4.) If my team is shortlisted for the on-site hackathon, will my team be reimbursed for expenses pertaining to travel?

-> No reimbursement would be provided. You’ll have to make your own arrangements.

5.) Once accepted, what should I bring?

-> Bring your student ID, laptop and chargers. A pillow and sleeping bag may be useful as well. Please leave anything illegal at home.

6.) Can I start working on my hack before the event?

-> No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before the hackathon begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand.

7.) How to reach Government Engineering College Aurangabad?

-> Aurangabad is well connected to all major cities of India by train and roads. The airport at Aurangabad has daily flights to Delhi/Hyderabad/Mumbai.

8.) Who is eligible to apply?

-> Students enrolled in any college in India can apply for the hackathon.
